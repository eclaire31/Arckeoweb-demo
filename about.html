We are Les Brickodeurs !

We are Les Brickodeurs, a french robotic club, that teachs robotic and programation to the childrens of our city, Saint-Graien.

Every year, our club participates a robotic competition called FLL (First Lego Leage), in which we have to build a robot, make missions an present a project.

This year, the FLL was about archeology.

We decided to create a kind of database of archeologicals models, in which people can share their archeological founds and see what others have found.

The whole project was made by the members of Les Brickodeurs.

We hope you will like it !
